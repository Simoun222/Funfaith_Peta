"Pilgrims of Hope" is the theme for the 2025 Jubilee year. The Jubilee year is a tradition that happens every 25 years. As expressed by Pope Francis, throughout the Jubilee year, we must always keep alive our hope and strength to share or encourage those who seek hope for a better future.

The late Pope Francis announced that Blessed Carlo Acutis and Blessed Pier Giorgio Frassati, two young Catholics beloved for their spirited faith and witness to holiness, will be canonized during two major Jubilee celebrations dedicated to young people. The canonization of Blessed Carlo Acutis was set originally for April 25, 2025 to April 27, 2025 during the Jubilee of Teenagers but has been moved to a later date. This is in accordance with the “Ordo Exsequiarum Romani Pontificis” or the “Rite of Burial for Roman Pontiffs.” The canonization of Blessed Pier Giorgio Frassati was set on July 28, 2025 to August 3, 2025 during the Jubilee of Youth.

In 2021, the Philippines marked 500 years of Christianity with the theme “Gifted to Give.” People celebrated their faith and the joy of giving to others. Even when the pandemic was ongoing, the event inspired Filipinos to continue their spirit journey and charitable works for future generations.
